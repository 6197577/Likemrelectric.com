Why is an Electrical Inspection Important?

A thorough electrical inspection is crucial for the safety and security of your property. Whether you're buying a new home, renovating, or just want peace of mind, our certified electricians will conduct a detailed assessment of your entire electrical system. We identify potential hazards, code violations, and inefficiencies that could lead to fires, electrical shock, or costly repairs down the line.

Our Inspection Process Includes:

  • Checking for outdated wiring (e.g., knob and tube, aluminum).
  • Verifying proper grounding and surge protection.
  • Inspecting your electrical panel for safety and capacity.
  • Testing smoke detectors, carbon monoxide detectors, and GFCI/AFCI outlets.
  • Providing a detailed report of our findings and recommendations.
